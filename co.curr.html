Digital Electronis Engineering Project

In this project, we designed and implemented a Two 4-bit binary Number Adder circuit used to add two binary numbers. The circuit takes two 4-bit inputs and produces a 4-bit sum along with a carry output. I used basic digital logic components such as full adders and logic gates to build the system. The project helped me understand the working of binary addition, carry propagation, and how multiple full adders can be connected to perform higher-bit operations. It also gave me practical knowledge of circuit design, truth tables, and digital logic implementation, which are fundamental concepts in digital electronics and computer architecture.

Design Thinking Project

“I am currently developing a Carbon Footprint Calculator project FOOTPRINT FIGHTER that helps users estimate their daily environmental impact based on activities such as transportation, energy usage, and lifestyle choices. The aim of this project is to raise awareness about carbon emissions and promote sustainable habits by providing simple, practical green solutions. Through this, I am combining technology with environmental responsibility to create a user-friendly and impactful tool.”
